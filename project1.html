Sugar Rush 

Sugar Rush Speedway Mocktail Recipe Book is a pairing book based on the characters from the Disney film Wreck it Ralph. This cookbook offers delicious sugary drinks made solely of candy, cakes, and sweets coupled with each character from the Sugar Rush game. While the reader prepares for the most hyperactive drive to finish the drink recipes, this book takes them on a journey that makes them feel like they are in the
race themselves.
