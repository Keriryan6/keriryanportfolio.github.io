City of Cambridge 50th Logo 

This was a partnered project for the City of Cambridge to create a logo for their 2023 50th anniversary celebration. The logo is applied to things such as merchandise, promotional content, and environmental signs. The logo needed to encapsulate aspects that represented Preston, Hespeler
and Galt joining to become Cambridge. Our final logo was inspired by a mobius loop and the existing City of Cambridge logo. It has different elements that are intertwined and incorporate the concept of the three towns within Cambridge forming one city. Through multiple rounds of voting, the public decided our logo as the finalists. As such, we will see our logo is seen across Cambridge to celebrate their 50th anniversary.
