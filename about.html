About Me

Hi there! My name is Keri, and I am a second-year Graphic Design student at Conestoga College. I'm known for my bubbly and happy personality. My focus in design tends to lean into brand identity, illustration, typographic design, and motion graphics, but I am up to any project that comes my way! I love meeting and working with new people and collaborating with other designers. So feel free to connect with me about your next project. I'm looking
forward to working with you!
